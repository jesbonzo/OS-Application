Scheduling Algorithm

FIRST COME, FIRST SERVE (FCFS)

  • Also known as First In, First Out (FIFO)
  • Easy to understand and implement.
  • Arriving jobs are inserted into the tail of the ready queue and the process to execute next is removed from the head of the queue.
  • Jobs are scheduled in the order in which they are received.
  • Poor in performance as average wait time is high.

Example

Process AT BT
1 0 10
2 5 6
3 7 7
4 6 3
5 3 1

Solution:
Gantt chart:

Process
1 5 2 4 3

0 10 11 17 20 27

  • CPU = 27/27 x 100
    = 100%
  • ST = 5/27
  • TAT = End Time - Arrival Time
  • WT = Turnaround time - Burst Time
    • Process TAT WT
    1 10 - 0 = 10 10 - 10 = 0
    2 17 - 5 = 12 12 - 6 = 6
    3 27 - 7 = 20 20 - 7 = 13
    4 20 - 6 = 14 14 - 3 = 11
    5 11 - 3 = 8 8 -1 = 7
    • ATAT = 10+12+20+14+8/5
      = 64/5
      = 12.8
    • AWT = 0+6+13+11+7/5
      = 37/5
      = 7.4
